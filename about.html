We are the One who Got U Covered!

As we reintroduce your mind and body to Basic Training type of Activities for a Totally FUN and CHALLENGING Time

Research shows that when wounded veterans spend quality time participating in physical and mental activities,they are less likely to commit suicide or diminish in mental health. So, we have made it our goal to improve vets lives and combat suicide among our nation's veterans by designing various fun/challenging activities that have been tested and proven to "help the mind focus and help the body heal!"
